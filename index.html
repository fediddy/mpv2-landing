01The pipeline

Every stage runs headless, is idempotent, and persists to a single SQLite CRM. No moving parts you can't observe.

01 · SOURCE
Scrape
Google Maps scraper CSV → normalized lead rows
02 · ENRICH
Verify
Email guessing + MX validation, phone classification, real Google reviews & photos via RapidAPI
03 · GENERATE
Mockup
Per-lead Jinja2 render + Playwright screenshot from vetted Tailwind templates
04 · SEND
Deliver
SMTP with tracked opens & clicks, per-lead subject & body render
05 · REPLIES
Classify
IMAP fetch + LLM classifier: interested / bounce / auto-reply / spam. Pause sequences, suppress bounces, notify.

03Under the hood

What most cold-outreach tools skip because it's hard.

Bespoke per-lead, not templated blast

Every mockup uses the target's real business name, city, license number, reviews, hero photo. The prospect sees their own information, not a generic template with a name-swap.

Reply intelligence, not just sends

Full IMAP loop. RFC 3464 bounce parsing. LLM classifier (interested / bounce / auto-reply / spam). Sequences pause automatically on hard-bounces. Operator gets pinged on hot replies.

Deliverability discipline

MX-validated addresses only. Landline vs. mobile classification before any SMS. Tracked opens/clicks per lead. Suppression list persisted across campaigns.

Idempotent + observable

Every stage is a rerunnable script. Single SQLite CRM. Migrations versioned (001 → 004+). If a stage crashes at scale, replay only what didn't ship.

Voice follow-up (VAPI)

Prospects who open but don't reply get an AI voice call from a per-vertical Vapi assistant configured against their business — 14 niches covered with locked prompt suites.

Extracted from a larger system

Forked from MoneyPrinterV2's outreach module and rebuilt as a standalone, production-grade pipeline. What's here is what actually runs — no dead code, no vaporware demo.
